PVC rated cables

PVC cables deals with what's on the outside of the cable. Nothing to do with the shielding nor wiring. PVC cables have 3 different types: Plenum, Riser and CM (general use)

Plenum cables

Plenum cables should be used in plenum areas. Plenum areas are when there is open air circulation. Due to the air circulation this allows for fires. Plenum cables are fire resistant and do not produce toxic fumes.

Riser (CMR)

Riser cables are used to rise between floors. they prevent fires between floors.

CM General Use

Used for general use, mostly used to connect to computers and routers.
