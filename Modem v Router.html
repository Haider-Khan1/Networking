Modem

A modem is what brings the internet into your home or business. The reason we have a modem is due to two different signals that are used on a computer an on the internet. Internet reads analog signals while computer read digital signals. People who only want to connect 1 device can just use a modem, no need for a router.

Router

Now there are a multitude of routers used for specific reasons. There are more powerful routers for big businesses or there are standard ones for homes or offices. A router allows multiple devices have the ability to connect to the internet. The router is connected to the ISP (Internet Service Provider) which gives you the network to connect to the net.

Different types of modems

The most common modems are cable and DSL. Cable modems are good for homes and are provider through your ISP. DSL modems are connect using a typical phone line. Comapnies such as AT&T offer these types of modems. Sometimes when you buy/recive a modem from your ISP they can give you a router/modem. This is a modem with a built in router in one physical device.
